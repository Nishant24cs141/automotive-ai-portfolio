
# Personal Portfolio for NISHANT J - CSE Student

## Design Overview
A sleek, dark-themed portfolio inspired by the DVxUI design with:
- **Dark background** (#0a0a0a / near-black)
- **Warm orange/coral accent** (#FF6B35 or similar) for highlights
- **3D-style avatar illustration** (placeholder with option to add custom image)
- **Glassmorphism card effects** with subtle borders
- **Smooth scroll-triggered animations** throughout

---

- Logo/Name: "nishant." (lowercase, stylized)

## Animations & Interactivity
- Fade-in animations on scroll (using CSS/JS)
